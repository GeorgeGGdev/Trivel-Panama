Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and travel experiences

General Questions

What is Trivel Panama?

Trivel Panama is a specialized travel agency dedicated to showcasing the most beautiful destinations in Panama. We create unique and memorable experiences for our clients, from paradise beaches to jungle adventures.

Where are you located?

Our main office is located in San Miguelito, Calle E. We also have representatives in different tourist destinations throughout the country to better serve our clients.

Do you offer services in other languages?

Yes, we offer services in both Spanish and English. Our team is trained to assist tourists from different nationalities and provide excellent multilingual support.

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. You can also contact us via WhatsApp 24/7 for emergencies.

Reservations & Bookings

How far in advance should I make my reservation?

We recommend making reservations at least 2 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (December-April). For popular destinations, booking 3-4 weeks ahead is ideal.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation up to 48 hours before the trip. Refund policies vary depending on the package type and destination.

What documents do I need to make a reservation?

You will need: valid passport (for foreigners), national ID (for Panamanians), contact information, and payment method. For special destinations, additional documents may be required.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special discounts for groups of 6 or more people. We also have seasonal promotions and special rates for families. Contact us for more information.

Destinations & Places

What are your most popular destinations?

Our most popular destinations include: Bocas del Toro, San Blas, Playa Venao, Isla Grande, Taboga Island, Contadora Island, and the Panama Canal. Each offers unique experiences and unforgettable memories.

What is the best time to visit each destination?

The dry season (December-April) is ideal for most destinations. Bocas del Toro is best from September to November, while San Blas is perfect year-round. We'll advise you based on your preferences.

Are the destinations safe for tourists?

Yes, all our destinations are safe for tourists. We work with trusted local partners and maintain high safety standards. Your security and comfort are our top priorities.

Do you offer customized tours?

Absolutely! We create personalized itineraries based on your interests, budget, and available time. From extreme adventures to relaxing getaways, we adapt everything to your needs.

Payments & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, cash payments at our office, and major credit cards. We also offer installment payment options for your convenience.

Do prices include all services?

Contact us and we'll provide you with a detailed breakdown of what's included in each package. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans to make your dream vacation more accessible. You can pay in installments leading up to your travel date.

Are there any additional fees?

We provide transparent pricing with all fees clearly stated upfront. Any additional costs will be communicated before booking confirmation.

Travel Information

What should I pack for my trip?

We provide a specific packing list for each destination. Generally, include: comfortable clothing, sunscreen, insect repellent, documents, personal medications, and a camera.

What happens if there's bad weather?

We constantly monitor weather conditions. If there's bad weather, we offer to reschedule your trip at no additional cost or provide a full refund. Your safety is our priority.

Do the guides speak English?

Yes, all our guides are certified and speak both Spanish and English fluently. They are knowledgeable about local culture and history.

What if I have special needs?

We accommodate special needs such as reduced mobility, specific diets, allergies, etc. Please inform us in advance so we can prepare everything appropriately.

Didn't find your answer?

Our team is here to help you with any additional questions you may have about our services.
