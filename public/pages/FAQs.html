Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and travel experiences

General Questions

What is Trivel Panama?

Trivel Panama is a specialized travel agency dedicated to showcasing the most beautiful destinations in Panama. We create unique and memorable experiences for our clients, from paradise beaches to jungle adventures.

Where are you located?

Our main office is located in San Miguelito, Calle E. We also have representatives in different tourist destinations throughout the country to better serve our clients.

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of services including guided tours, accommodation booking, transportation, adventure activities, and personalized travel packages to the most beautiful destinations in Panama.

Do you offer services in multiple languages?

Yes, we offer services in Spanish and English. Our guides are bilingual and we can accommodate requests for other languages with advance notice.

Reservations & Booking

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for popular destinations, especially during peak season (December to April). For custom packages, we suggest booking 6-8 weeks in advance.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies. Most bookings can be cancelled up to 48 hours before the tour date for a full refund. Some special packages may have different terms which will be clearly communicated.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 24 hours before the tour date, subject to availability. Contact us directly and we'll help you make the necessary changes.

Destinations & Tours

What are your most popular destinations?

Our most popular destinations include Bocas del Toro, San Blas Islands, Playa Venao, Isla Grande, and Panama City. Each offers unique experiences from pristine beaches to cultural immersion.

Do you offer custom tours?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom tours tailored to your preferences, group size, and interests. Contact us to discuss your dream Panama adventure.

What's included in your tours?

Our tours typically include transportation, professional guides, activities, and often meals. Specific inclusions vary by tour and are clearly detailed in each package description.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and cash payments. For online bookings, we use secure payment gateways to ensure your information is protected.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 6 or more people. Contact us for group pricing and we'll create a customized package for your group.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! All our prices are transparent and include taxes. Any additional costs (like optional activities) will be clearly communicated upfront.

Travel Information

What should I bring on tours?

We recommend bringing comfortable clothing, sunscreen, water, camera, and any personal items. Specific requirements vary by tour and will be provided in your confirmation.

What's the best time to visit Panama?

Panama has a tropical climate. The dry season (December to April) is ideal for beach activities, while the rainy season (May to November) offers lush landscapes and fewer crowds.

Do I need travel insurance?

While not mandatory, we strongly recommend travel insurance for international visitors. It provides peace of mind and protection against unexpected events.
